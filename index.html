Species occurrences in nature reserves

Search for a taxon, pick a country or the whole world, and optionally restrict to protected areas — then explore GBIF occurrences overlaid on OpenStreetMap reserve boundaries. GBIF data is converted to RDF via gbif_parquet and queried live on QLever. Built as a demo for the Ebbe Nielsen Challenge 2026.

Select a taxon and country, then “Map it”.
